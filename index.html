About Us

Pamushana Zimbabwe United Nations Association is a youth-driven club focused on building responsible, active students who are passionate about improving their school and helping those in need. We believe in leadership, unity, and development through action.

Events

Our club organizes fundraising events and supports local school development initiatives. Through these activities, we raise resources and bring positive change to our school community.
